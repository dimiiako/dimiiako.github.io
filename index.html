EXPERIENCE

Don't stop until you're proud!

My engineering experience began with working voluntary as member of 2 student teams in 2014 when I was on the third year of my studies. In Aristurtle (www.aristurtle.gr) the goal was to build an electrical formula and I was the battery-boy, I designed the battery box and the electrical system configuration on the chassis of the formula. I worked on the team for a year and I am proud being part of this effort.

In Prognosis, (http://prognosisproject.net) under the guidance of our mentor, we worked on creating a startup that aimed to the Early detection of Parkinson's disease and building supportive interventions . The concept is to analyse data captured from patients and healthy in order to build an unobtrusive tool aiming to detect behavioural changes through smart devices and build tools that could sustain the quality of life of the patients. I felt really nice working on this area and I found my passion not only because it is challenging as an engineering problema but it can have a huge impact to the community.

After my graduation I started working remotely as consulting in Kaia Health a software startup that aiming to build software for rehabilitation therapies. Currently, I started my PhD and I am working as a Research associate in i-Prognosis a research programme funded by Horizon 2020 which is the research part of the Prognosis idea. I analyse behavioral information with machine learning in python and developing software for the mobile application.
